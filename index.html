Privacy Policy

DopeMode — Productivity Timer App

Last updated: March 4, 2026

DopeMode is a fully offline app. No data ever leaves your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

DopeMode stores the following data locally on your device using on-device storage (MMKV):

  • Task objectives — short text you enter to describe your focus goal (max 80 characters)
  • Session history — timestamps, durations, and outcomes of your focus sessions (up to 500 entries)
  • Preferences — your chosen settings such as sound/haptic toggles, sprint and break durations, and daily session goals
  • Onboarding status — whether you have completed the introductory tutorial

Data We Do NOT Collect

DopeMode does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No network requests or internet communication
  • No user accounts, logins, or authentication
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, photos, or other device data

Third-Party Services

DopeMode does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete all session history at any time from the Settings screen using the "Clear History" button. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

DopeMode does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
